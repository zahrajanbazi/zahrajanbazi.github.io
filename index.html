Fatemehzahra Janbazi

Ph.D. Candidate, Department of Mathematics, University of Toronto

I work in arithmetic statistics, arithmetic invariant theory, and arithmetic geometry — focusing on finiteness theorems for orbits of forms, Selmer group statistics, and geometric models such as K3 surfaces and rational elliptic surfaces.

Office: Bahen Centre, 40 St George St, Toronto, ON · Email: zahra.janbazi@mail.utoronto.ca
